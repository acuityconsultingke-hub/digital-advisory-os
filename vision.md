# DAOS Vision

A holistic, Kenya-native Digital Advisory Operating System (DAOS) designed for financial advisors, insurers, and future SaaS deployment.

Key goals:
- Advisor augmentation, not replacement
- Deterministic, explainable advice
- Kenya-context first (MPESA, SACCOs, NHIF)
- White-label ready for insurers (Britam, Jubilee, etc.)
